FocusPal doesn't push you to work harder. It helps you understand why you're stuck — and meets you exactly where you are.

Evidence-informed approaches

Matched to your feeling.

Different emotions need different tools. FocusPal routes you to the right approach — automatically.

👁️
Anxious / Fearful

ACT · Cognitive Defusion

Separate yourself from anxious thoughts. You are not the fear — you are the one noticing it.

🌺
Guilty / Self-Blaming

Ho'oponopono · Zero Limits

A Hawaiian forgiveness practice. Release self-blame and return to a place of peace.

🫂
Overwhelmed / Stressed

Self-Compassion · Kristin Neff

Acknowledge the pain, recognise shared humanity, and offer yourself kindness.

🍃
Frustrated / Resistant

Zen Mindfulness

Observe resistance without judgment. Let the energy move through you rather than against you.
