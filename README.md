# FocusPal — Emotional Insight

> Maybe you're not lazy. Maybe you're just stuck.

FocusPal is a Chrome extension that helps students understand what's really blocking them when they procrastinate — and guides them back with self-compassion.

## What it does

When you've been idle for a moment, FocusPal gently appears and walks you through a short emotional check-in:

1. **Feel Seen** — Name what you're feeling right now
2. **Understand** — Uncover the hidden belief underneath the block
3. **Reframe** — A short, evidence-informed reset matched to your emotion
4. **Move Forward** — One small, kind step back to your task

## Four emotional paths

| Feeling | Approach |
|---|---|
| 😨 Anxious / Fearful | ACT · Cognitive Defusion |
| 😔 Guilty / Self-Blaming | Ho'oponopono · Zero Limits |
| 🤯 Overwhelmed / Stressed | Self-Compassion · Kristin Neff |
| 😤 Frustrated / Resistant | Zen Mindfulness |

## Privacy

- No personal data collected
- Session data stored locally on your device
- Only anonymous, aggregated data sent for academic research
- No ads. No accounts. No tracking.

## Research

FocusPal is an academic research prototype studying emotional micro-interventions and procrastination in students.
